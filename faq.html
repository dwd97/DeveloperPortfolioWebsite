Some basic troubleshooting steps include:

Restarting the device. (No data will be lost)
  • Hold the power button and slide to power off.
  • Wait for 10 seconds and turn it back on.
Clearing cache or reinstalling app. (Data will be lost, please back it up.)
  • On Android: Navigate to settings, apps, select your app and tap clear cache or clear data.
  • On iOS/iPadOS/MacOS: The app needs to be reinstalled. Delete the app and then navigate to app store to delete the app.
